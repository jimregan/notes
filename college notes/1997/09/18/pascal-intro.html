Introduction to Programming

Computer – programmable electronic device that can store information, process it and display results

Hardware – Any physical component

Software – set of instruction which enables computer to perform functions

History

  • Newgrange, abacus – 2000 years

  • Logarithms – 1500 years

  • Pascal’s adder, Leibnitz calculator – 1600s

  • Jacquard’s Loom – 1801

  • Difference machine, Analytical engine – 1820s

  • Hollerith equipment – 1900

  • Mark 1 – 1944

  • Atanasoff Berry Computer – 1942
